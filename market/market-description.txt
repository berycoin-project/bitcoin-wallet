Have your Berycoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Berycoin Wallet is the first mobile Berycoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Berycoin amount in BERY and BeryFlowers.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Berycoin via NFC, QR-codes or Berycoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Berycoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Berycoin balance.

You can find more about Berycoin at
http://berycoin.org

If you want to contribute to Berycoin Wallet, the project is located at
